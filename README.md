# 🍽️ Restaurant Business Website

A professional website developed for a local restaurant to improve its online presence and help customers easily access information about the business.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on building a simple and responsive website for a real local restaurant business.  
The goal is to demonstrate how a website can help small businesses attract more customers and grow digitally.

---

## 🎯 Project Objective

Many local businesses do not have an online platform where customers can view their menu, services, or contact details.

This website solves that problem by providing:

- Business information
- Menu details
- Gallery of food and restaurant
- Contact details for customers

---

## 🛠️ Technologies Used

- HTML
- CSS
- JavaScript

---

## 🌟 Website Features

✔️ Home Page – Introduction to the restaurant  
✔️ About Page – Information about the business  
✔️ Menu Page – Food items available  
✔️ Gallery Page – Images of food and restaurant  
✔️ Contact Page – Address, phone number and contact form  

---

---

## 💼 Business Impact

This website helps the business by:

- Increasing online visibility
- Making it easier for customers to view the menu
- Providing contact information and location
- Creating a professional digital presence
